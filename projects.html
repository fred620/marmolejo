TaskMaster

TaskMaster is a task management app developed using React and Node.js. It enables users to efficiently create, update, and organize their daily tasks, complete with priority levels and deadlines. Featuring user authentication and real-time updates, TaskMaster offers a clean, responsive interface designed to enhance productivity and streamline task tracking.

SAVEWINS

SaveWins is a mobile application developed using Flutter that helps users monitor and track their daily habits and personal goals. Designed with a user-friendly interface, SaveWins encourages positive behavior by providing insightful progress reports and reminders to keep you motivated on your journey toward self-improvement.

AutoTrack

AutoTrack is a vehicle maintenance tracking app built with Flutter and Firebase. It allows users to log service history, set reminders for oil changes, tire rotations, and inspections, helping car owners keep their vehicles in top condition.
